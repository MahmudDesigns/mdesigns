Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how Mahmud Designs uses cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our website. It explains what these technologies are and why we use them, as well as your rights to control their use.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files stored on your computer or device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites function more efficiently and provide reporting information.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to improve your experience on our website. They help us understand how visitors interact with the site, remember your preferences, and deliver relevant content.

3. Types of Cookies

  • Essential cookies: Required for the website to function properly.
  • Performance cookies: Help us measure website performance and user behavior.
  • Functional cookies: Remember your preferences and settings.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect how the website functions.

5. Third-Party Cookies

We may allow trusted third parties to place cookies for analytics or functionality purposes. These are governed by their own privacy policies.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. We encourage you to review it regularly.
